# Power BI Report Pages – Domino’s Analytics Project

This Power BI report is built using the Kaggle `pizza_sales.csv` dataset and follows a clean, business‑focused layout. Each page aligns with a real stakeholder need.

---

## 1. Sales & Product Performance Dashboard

### Purpose
Provide leadership and marketing teams with a clear view of revenue, product mix, and sales trends.

### Visuals
- **KPI Cards**
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Orders
  - Average Order Value (AOV)

- **Column Chart**
  - Revenue by Month (sorted by MonthNumber)

- **Bar Chart**
  - Top 10 Pizzas by Revenue

- **Pie Chart**
  - Revenue by Category

- **Line Chart**
  - Revenue Trend Over Time

### Slicers
- Year
- Month
- Category
- Size
